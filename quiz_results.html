Question 1: In what year was the first Earth Day celebrated?

Correct Answer: 1970
*The first Earth Day took place on April 22, 1970.*


Question 2: Which of the following is NOT one of the 'Three Rs' of sustainability?

Correct Answer: Regenerate
*The three Rs are Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle.*


Question 3: What is the primary gas responsible for the greenhouse effect and climate change?

Correct Answer: Carbon Dioxide
*While Methane is also a strong greenhouse gas, Carbon Dioxide (CO2) is the primary driver of climate change globally due to its volume.*
