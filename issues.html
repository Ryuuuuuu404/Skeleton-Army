Major Environmental Issues

1. Climate Change

Climate change is caused by the increase of greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide. This leads to rising temperatures, melting ice caps, extreme weather, and rising sea levels.

2. Air Pollution

Air pollution is caused by factories, vehicles, and open burning. It contributes to health problems like asthma and lung disease.

3. Deforestation

Forests are being cut down for agriculture, construction, and logging. This destroys habitats, increases carbon dioxide, and threatens biodiversity.

4. Water Pollution

Water sources become polluted due to industrial waste, sewage, and plastics. Contaminated water affects both humans and marine animals.
