About This Website

This Earth Day Awareness website was created to educate, inspire, and promote a deeper understanding of the importance of environmental protection. Our mission is to encourage individuals, especially students and young people, to take active steps toward creating a more sustainable and healthier planet.

We aim to provide clear, accessible, and engaging information about topics such as the history of Earth Day, environmental issues, sustainable practices, and positive actions that anyone can take. Through this platform, we hope to spread awareness and empower communities to protect the environment for future generations.

Our Objectives:
  • To educate the public about the origins and purpose of Earth Day.
  • To highlight major environmental challenges affecting our planet.
  • To inspire sustainable habits and eco-friendly lifestyles.
  • To showcase global and local efforts to protect the environment.
  • To encourage student and community participation in environmental activities.

Who We Are

We are Group The 3 Mousketeers, a team of students passionate about environmental education and conservation. This website is part of our assignment project, aimed at creating a resourceful and meaningful learning platform.
