DIY Projects

DIY eco-friendly projects encourage sustainable living by reducing waste and reusing old materials. Examples include making compost bins using recycled containers, crafting vertical gardens using plastic bottles, repurposing glass jars as storage, and creating natural cleaners from vinegar and lemon. These simple, low-cost projects help reduce pollution and encourage greener habits at home.
